Attention Is All You Need

By Ashish Vaswani, et al.

This seminal paper introduces the Transformer architecture, a novel network architecture based solely on attention mechanisms, dispensing with recurrence and convolutions entirely.

Highly accurate protein structure prediction with AlphaFold

By John Jumper, et al.

Presents AlphaFold, an AI system that predicts protein 3D structure from its amino acid sequence, a significant breakthrough in computational biology.

Reducing the Dimensionality of Data with Neural Networks

By G. E. Hinton & R. R. Salakhutdinov

A classic paper demonstrating how deep autoencoder networks can be used for effective dimensionality reduction, outperforming traditional methods like PCA.

DALL·E: Creating Images from Text

By OpenAI

An exploration into creating a version of GPT-3 trained to generate images from text descriptions, using a dataset of text-image pairs.
